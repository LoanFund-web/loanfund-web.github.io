Crowfunding for Loans

LoanFund is the first global platform dedicated to:

1. consumers who are in need of a loan and who are tired of paying astronomical fees from banks

2. Investors who are looking to gain on the upside of the loan market without fully committing their capital for the entirety of the loan.

How Does It Work?

  • Loan Sharing Platform

    - LoanFund is the like a Crowfunding platform for loans, where investors can contribute to the loans of our ever growing number of consumers.

  • Consumers

    - Loans can be posted for any given amount and these loans can be matched by any number of our LoanFund investors who will contribute to a certain percentage of the overall loan.

    - This entails more liquidity and less costs of fees and interest per loan.

  • Investors

    - Contributing any given percentage to the loan of each user will result in access to new levels of returns and markets.

    - Access to a tailor made database of fully credit rated borrowers which we will match to your exact risk preferences.

    - This entails greater transparency and efficiency in the loan servicing process.
